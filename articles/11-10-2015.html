Annual Italian Heritage Parade

A big parade was held in San Francisco today. It’s called Italian Heritage Parade. Tomorrow, 2015-Oct-12 is Columbus day which celebrates the Columbus’s arrival in America. Columbus is Italian so it’s important for many Italian-American people. The parade was in district where many Italian people live. In the parade, there were many people including children, adult, the elderly, Asian, Negroid and Caucasian.

Each community which participates in the parade do their own performance. It was also interesting that they are different from group to group. In a group, a lot of people danced with pop-music and in another group, people played very accurate marching band like army. In a LGBT community, a middle aged man did batten performance with sparkling costume.

In this event, I felt the diversity in San Francisco again. Although a lot of different people gather, they can enjoy together.
