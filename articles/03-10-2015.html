What a Japanese student felt in Silicon Valley

Okonomiyaki

I cooked okonomiyaki and misosoup with wakame and aburage for the host family. I don’t know why but okonomiyaki was too soft so the appearance was not good but they liked it.

I realized that they say they like Japanese food but they don’t know much about Japanese food. A lot of ingredients used in it is not known. I explained about nagaimo, katsuobushi, wakame and aburage. It was very interesting. And they usually make misosoup without dashi so I taught azhinomoto is very useful.

I have been really helped by them so I hope it replayed to a certain extent.
