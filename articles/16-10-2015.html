What a Japanese student felt in Silicon Valley

People from the same country

The number of students from Japan in my school is increasing gradually. It isn’t necessarily bad. Honestly, they are easier to talk to. But some people speak Japanese so I don’t like it. I can’t understand why they speak their native language at English school in the U.S. I know the life in which you have to be soaked into the language you are not used to is but it must not be avoidable to master English.

The teacher told us not to use our mother tongues in front of all students so I expected to be able to keep distance from Japanese but situation is getting worse. I sometimes protect my ear by headphone but I can’t do so all the time. I want to find some friends who can speak English well but I’m avoiding some people who speak Japanese so it’s a little difficult. I’m feeling my English is getting better a little by little so I want keep it somehow.
