Bar

I went to a bar yesterday with friends in my school. Monday is a service day so it was very reasonable. The price is one dollar per one drink!! Taste was also good so the cost performance was amazing.

It is in Castro where many latin people live. Everyone was very open minded. People are very friendly so it was really fun. I also talked a lot with friends in the same school and we can be more friendly. This kind of break is also important for my life so I will go sometimes.
