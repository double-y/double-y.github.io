What a Japanese student felt in Silicon Valley

Academy of Science

I went to a science museum in San Francisco with a friend of mine. Its name is California Academy of Science. Normally, it costs about $30 (about 3000 yen) but today was a special day called neighborhood day. It is a day for people who lives near from it so it was free for us.

It has aquarium, planetarium, hothouse whose inside is similar to Rainforest and exhibitions. The roof is called “Living Roof” and covered with plants. Every content was interesting.

Compared to science museum in Japan, I felt the the characteristics was the variety. In Japan, I don’t know any facility which has aquarium with scientific exhibitions. However, they are well organized as one museum. I admired the design of it.
