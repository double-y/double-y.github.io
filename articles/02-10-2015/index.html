October 2, 2015

Transportation of San Francisco

Because of delay of train, I couldn’t get to school in time so I’ll write about transportation system of San Francisco.

In San Francisco, there’s three big transportation system. Bus, subway, and train. Although there are many choices compared to other place in the U.S. but system is not good. There’s a lot of delay and they sometimes don’t announce. Today, boards said the train is operating on schedule but it arrived 20 minutes later. I had to wait in cold morning. Moreover, You can enter a platform without checking IC card or ticket. I first thought it’s free. Seeing other people are putting their IC card, I noticed my mistake but if there had been no other passenger, I might have mistake. The system is not good in terms of fail safe.

I think the reason why it’s not good is people usually use cars but a lot of people from other place like me often use public transportation so I want the system to be easier to understand.
