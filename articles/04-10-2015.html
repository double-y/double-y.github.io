What a Japanese student felt in Silicon Valley

Home Party

My host family hold a home party every Sunday. A lot of their family live around the bay area so many people came. About 10 adults and 10 their children. They are very smart people. A lawyer of Fac*book, business person in ad*be, engineer who is working for a chip company, etc. Moreover, they are very kind and friendly. They are great people.

Food and drink were very nice too. One of a guest brought nice wine given by some customers. Dishes were grilled salmon, stake , grilled asparagus and spinach pie. They were all amazing. I ate too much. I started to worry about my weight.
