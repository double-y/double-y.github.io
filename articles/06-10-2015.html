Mexican Food

In the U.S., Mexican food is very popular. There are a lot of restaurants which serves it. In super markets, Mexican foodstuffs like tortilla, tako and salsa.

In California, it is also common but they are usually arranged. Various kind of ingredients are available so they use them which doesn’t exist in Mexico. For example, I ate enchilada which wraps some mushrooms and spinach but original one usually contains meet and beans. It was very nice.

It is a very typical Californian thing. People all over the world gathers here so cultures are mixed. They don’t stick to tradition if they can improve it. I like such mentality.
