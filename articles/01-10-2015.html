What a Japanese student felt in Silicon Valley

Burning Man

In our class, sometimes students make a presentation. The topic can be anything. Today, one of my classmates who is studying metal sculpture made one about Burning Man Festival. It’s an art festival which is held in a desert in end of August. The most interesting part for me was participants live without money for one week. People don’t sell and buy anything. They just give.

This sounds like communism but I think it’s different. Probably, this festival is not trying to change the system of society although communism is. This is just a trial to find an utopia. They know it doesn’t last forever but they also can’t stop dreaming about it. Interestingly, it is art festival. They are trying to go beyond this society with art which have immeasurable power.

I feel something interesting and important from this festival so I want to go there someday.
