Rain

September 30, 2015

Rain

Today, it was rainy. If I was in Japan, it would be not rare but I’m in San Francisco so it really is. In San Francisco, it has not been rainy for 3 years. Because of it, farmers were in trouble. People are happy thanks to the rain. Japanese people usually don’t like rain so it’s interesting for me.

In addition, if you are not farmer, it has a merit. Air in San Francisco is dusty because there is a desert windward. Thankfully, rain cleanse it. Therefore, the air tomorrow will be clean and refreshing.
