Education

December 6, 2015

I feel that more and more people are talking about the importance of education these days. Edutech is a buzz word in silicon valley. It is a compound word of education and technology. How to use technology in education is a big discussion topic all over the world.

Honestly speaking, I don’t like that topic. I know it is important and I like thinking about it. The reason why I don’t like the topic is it is often considered as a magic field. People tend to expect it too much and impose too big responsibility to it. Perfect education may make people and society perfect but it can’t be realized. Moreover, many people think that teaching something to children is education. It’s too arrogant. You can help someone to learn but making someone study something is usually meaningless.

In my opinion, what you can do is only making a good environment where people can find motivations to start learning and try not to stop it as much as possible. You can’t directly change someone so easily. Changing environment and system is usually much easier and more efficient. You should make many triggers that will make people want to start learning rather than pushing it directly. If they find the reason to start learning, it should not be prevented. However, there are often still many obstacles. It can be the lack of good teachers, it can be no access to information and it can be the difficulty of understanding.

Thinking about how to help people to find out the reason to learn and how to ged rid of obstacles in front of them are very important. I’m thinking that technology can be used for those purposes. It is one of big themes which I should think in Silicon Valley because it can be a big opportunity to make a product.

I think that this world will be better if more people start learning. The problem is not how to teach. It is how to help them to realize the interesting aspects.
